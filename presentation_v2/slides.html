00. Executive Summary: The Challenge

Our client — a PE-backed business — faces a fundamental misalignment between Sales Promises (Revenue Contracts) and Supplier Realities (Purchase Contracts).

LEAKAGE TYPE 1

Price Mismatches

Selling a service at a price that doesn't cover the fluctuating costs of third-party inputs.

Example
Customer locked at fixed annual price.
Supplier contract includes CPI-linked inflation clause.
→ Margin erodes each year.
LEAKAGE TYPE 2

SLA Exposure

Promising the customer an SLA that the supplier's contract doesn't support.

Example
Customer promised 99.9% uptime.
Supplier only guarantees 99.5%.
→ Penalty risk sits with us.

"This leakage is rarely detected in audits — it surfaces in financial results."

2a. The Matching Logic: Normalising Obligations

"We don't normalise labels — we normalise obligations."

From Text → Structured Obligation

Contracts never use the same language. Comparing text will always fail. Instead, an LLM extracts structured attributes from each clause.

SALES CONTRACT
"Premium Support"
↔
SUPPLIER CONTRACT
"Tier 1 Incident Response
with 24/7 coverage"
↓ LLM extracts structured attributes ↓
Canonical Obligation: Critical Support Tier
Service: Technical Support Coverage: 24/7 Response (P1): 30 min Channels: Phone + Portal Escalation: Yes

Now you can compare, calculate gaps, generate risk, and automate audit — without depending on labels.

Hybrid Normalisation Strategy

A Contract Ontology (also: Canonical Model, Service Taxonomy) that combines deterministic precision with AI recall.

1. Deterministic Layer
Dictionary rules, synonym maps, industry acronyms. High precision.
2. Semantic Layer (Embeddings)
Captures equivalence even with different language.
"Mission Critical Support" ≈ "Tier 1 Support" — High recall.
3. LLM Structured Extraction
Generates the structured "obligation card" with all comparable attributes.
4. Confidence Scoring
If confidence < threshold → human review. Enterprises demand explainability.
Without this layer → impossible to scale contract analysis.

4. Project Roadmap (MVP)

An MVP scoped to deliver measurable value in 6 weeks — focused on the two leakage types with the highest financial impact.

✓ MVP Scope (Weeks 1–6)

  • PDF ingestion from CRM + ERP (top 50 contracts)
  • LLM extraction of prices, inflation clauses, SLAs
  • Rule-based + embedding matching (ontology v1)
  • Auditor Workbench with HITL review queue
  • Mismatch dashboard with top-10 risk report

✗ Deferred (Phase 2+)

  • Full knowledge graph (many-to-many resolution)
  • Real-time deal-desk scoring (pre-signature)
  • NLQ dashboard for C-suite
  • Full ERP/CRM API integration (batch import first)
  • Automated renegotiation recommendations
PRIORITY 1 — HIGHEST ROI

Price Mismatches

Detect contracts where cost exceeds revenue due to supplier price changes not reflected in sales pricing.

Success Metric:
Identify ≥£X in recoverable margin within the first 50 contracts.
PRIORITY 2 — COMPOUNDING RISK

Inflation Clause Gaps

Flag supplier contracts with CPI-linked escalations that aren't matched by customer price adjustments.

Success Metric:

PRIORITY 3 — LIABILITY EXPOSURE

SLA Exposure

Identify where customer SLA promises exceed supplier guarantees (e.g., 99.9% vs 99.5% uptime).

Success Metric:
Flag all SLA gaps with penalty-risk quantification.
